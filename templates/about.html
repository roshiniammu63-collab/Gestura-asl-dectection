About Gestura

Gestura is a deep learning-powered real-time American Sign Language (ASL) alphabet recognition system designed to bridge communication gaps between hearing-impaired individuals and the wider community.

Built using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and computer vision techniques, the system captures live hand gestures through a webcam and accurately translates them into readable text.

By combining artificial intelligence with inclusive design, Gestura promotes accessibility, social inclusion, and seamless interaction in everyday communication.

This project demonstrates the practical application of deep learning in assistive technology, offering a scalable foundation for future advancements in real-time sign language interpretation systems.
